--- This file will contain information about George Brown college ----

Goerge Brown College is named after George Brown who was one of the fathers of confederation. 
He also owned the Toronto Globe, which is what the Globe and mail used to be called back in the day.
George Brown College was established during the formation of Ontario's comunity college system in 1967.

Goerge Brown College has a athletics and sports division called the Goerg Brown Huskies.
The following are the sports offered by goerge brown.

Badminton - M & W
Baseball - M
Basketball - M
Basketball - W
Soccer - M
Soccer - W
Indoor Soccer - M
Indoor Soccer - W
Volleyball - M
Volleyball - W
